Benefits of Speed Writing

Increased Automaticity

Lessens the burden on working memory. This means that you don’t have to actively concentrate on the act of writing itself, and that you can instead focus on thinking about what to write.

Increased Overlap

Between the mental generation of output and the consequent production of text relating to that output. This means that you can write your thoughts down immediately as you are forming them, without suffering from a delay which hinders your thought process.

Improved Performance

Improved performance in various academic tasks, and especially those that require a lot of handwriting under time constraints, such as taking notes during lectures or writing essays during in-class exams.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Add Ons?

Well, these are designed and crafted to get the most out of the training sessions that are covered in the Basic Course. Students will be provided with customized online tool which covers the topic covered in his level. This will give him/her enough practice each month to solve numerous sums each different from the other. Marks get regularly updated in our database. Parent's can track how their kids are performing from customized Analytical Dashboard specially developed for them. Now it becomes easy for you and the teacher to check which area to focus for improvement. Add Ons need to be purchase separately and comes at a discounted package if Premium course is selected.

Why the Add Ons come at extra charge?

Add Ons are specially designed in-house by us. Not very sure if this is available with any other Abacus center. This is developed after lot of research and has proven result on our existing students. We require to maintain the daily records of the students and provide real-time analytical dashboard customized for each student who have enrolled for the Premium course. Web Server and development charges add on to the cost.

How will I get the study kit?

We will ship the study kit and other materials at your mailing address. Flat shipping charge of ₹ 200 for students in Karnataka and ₹ 300 anywhere in India.
Shipping charges are free for Premium course and chargeable for Basic course.

What if my kid is unable to attend a class or more?

We know that inspite of being regular and dedicated, sometimes we cannot but skip avoiding the class because of a birthday party or an exam the next day. Do not worry, let the teacher know before hand and she will do the necessary arranagement. Ofcourse we do not entertain students who makes a habit of skipping classes unnecessarily ;)
Please note we provide only 1 compensation class per month for basic course and 2 for premium course.

What if I have opted for Basic course and want to upgrade to Premium. Is this possible?

Yes, it is very much possible. Upgrade and downgrade are both possible. When upgrading you will get the student login, dashboard, practice tools, monthly assessment, extra classes to speed up etc. And if you downgrade, all these features would be unavailable.

What are "extra classes"?

If a teacher finds a student requires some extra support to understand a concept, she will provide an extra class.
This is a premium only feature.
